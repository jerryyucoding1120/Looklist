Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how LookList uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website or use our app.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and login status.

2. How we use cookies

  • To enable essential site functionality, such as authentication and session management.
  • To improve your experience by remembering your preferences.
  • To analyze site usage and performance.
  • To display relevant content and offers.

3. Types of cookies we use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic platform operations.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze usage to improve features.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Used by our partners for analytics and payment processing.

4. Managing cookies

You may block or delete cookies in your browser settings. However, some features may not work if cookies are disabled.

5. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy. Updates will be posted on this page and are effective immediately.

6. Contact

If you have questions about our Cookie Policy, contact us at support@looklist.co.uk.
